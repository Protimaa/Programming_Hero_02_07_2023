Ratargul Swamp Forest is a freshwater swamp forest located in Gowainghat, Sylhet, Bangladesh.This place is awesome. The river is beautifully calm. Journey to the forest by boat is a mesmerizing experience. at the dock is where you hire boat to take you up river once you get to the forest the scenery is so beautiful. you have the boat man singing songs and they even help you to take pictures at the half way point you can get out and walk the trail depending on what season you go.
